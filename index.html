About us

We are Australian Red Cross.
We are a movement of people, we help Australians act for humanity.
We walk respectfully with First Nations peoples, towards a brighter and more equitable future. When people come to Australia seeking safety, we help them make ends meet, settle into their new communities, and search for loved ones gone missing.
We help to promote respect for the laws of war, protecting humanity in times of war and preventing the misuse of the symbol that saves lives in armed conflict – the red cross emblem. And, we stand with people for whom life has been unjust.
Red Cross pays our respects to the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander custodians of the country where we work, and to Elders, past, present and emerging.
Learn about our Reconciliation Action Plan and how we can all make reconciliation real.
